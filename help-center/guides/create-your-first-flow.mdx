## Build a Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start new flow">
    Click "New Flow" in your Flyde project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add nodes">
    Drag nodes (e.g. Trigger, Transform, Output) from the left panel to the canvas.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect nodes">
    Drag lines to connect output ports to input ports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test flow">
    Click "Run" or "Preview" to see your flow in action.
  </Step>
</Steps>
