---
title: How do I start a new Flyde project?
description: Learn to create your first Flyde workspace.
---

## Steps

1. Open Flyde and sign in or create an account.
2. Click "New Project" on the dashboard.
3. Enter your project name.
4. Optional: Choose a template.
5. Click "Create Project".

## Next

Your workspace is ready. Start adding flows and documents from the side panel. Changes save automatically.
